# Privacy Policy for AutoNotate

**Effective Date:** June 23, 2026

## Overview

AutoNotate is designed with your privacy in mind. This app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All functionality runs entirely on your device.

## Location Data

AutoNotate requests access to your device's location solely to provide local weather information powered by Apple WeatherKit. This location data is:

- Used only in real time to fetch weather for your current area
- Never stored, logged, or recorded by AutoNotate
- Never transmitted to or collected by the app developer
- Never shared with any third parties outside of Apple's WeatherKit service

For information on how Apple handles location and weather data through WeatherKit, please refer to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

## User-Generated Content & File Storage

AutoNotate allows you to store files and content on your device. All files you create or import remain:

- Stored locally on your device only
- Never uploaded, transmitted, or accessed by the app developer
- Fully under your own control

We have no access to any files or content you store within AutoNotate.

## Data Collection

AutoNotate does not collect any personal information, including but not limited to:

- Names, emails, or account credentials
- Device identifiers or usage analytics
- Location history
- Files or user-generated content

## Third-Party Services

AutoNotate uses **Apple WeatherKit**, an Apple-provided framework. All data requests made through WeatherKit are governed by Apple's own privacy practices.

No other third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising frameworks are used in this app.

## Children's Privacy

AutoNotate does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes in the future, the updated policy will be posted at this URL with a revised effective date.
